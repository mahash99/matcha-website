Beginner-friendly ritual

How to prepare your matcha.

Whether you love it warm and creamy or iced and refreshing, matcha is simple to prepare once you know the flow.

Hot Matcha Latte

  1. Add 1 teaspoon of matcha powder to a bowl or cup.
  2. Add a small amount of warm water, not boiling.
  3. Whisk until smooth and slightly foamy.
  4. Add warm milk of your choice.
  5. Sweeten lightly if desired and enjoy slowly.

Iced Matcha Latte

  1. Add matcha powder and warm water to a cup.
  2. Whisk until the matcha is fully dissolved.
  3. Fill a glass with ice.
  4. Add cold milk of your choice.
  5. Pour matcha over the milk and enjoy your ritual.
